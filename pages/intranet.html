Internet Term 8

Intranet

This page explains the intranet using its definition, role, and analogy.

Definition

An intranet is a private network accessible only within an organization.

Role

It is used for internal communication, collaboration, and sharing resources.

Analogy

Like a private group chat only employees can access.
