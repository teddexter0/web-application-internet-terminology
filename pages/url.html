Internet Term 5

URL

This page explains the URL using its definition, role, and analogy.

Definition

A URL (Uniform Resource Locator) is the address used to locate a specific resource on the internet.

Role

It tells the browser where to find a resource and how to access it.

Analogy

Like a home address guiding you to a specific location.
