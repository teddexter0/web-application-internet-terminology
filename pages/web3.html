Internet Term 15

Web 3.0

This page explains Web 3.0 using its definition, role, and analogy.

Definition

Web 3.0 is the decentralized and intelligent web focused on data ownership and AI.

Role

It enables smarter, personalized, and trustless interactions.

Analogy

Like owning your own data instead of relying on big platforms.
